The hands behind Beauty Zone

Meet Cinthia Padilla

Cinthia started working at 16, training as an esthetician long before she had a business of her own. As a single mother building a new life in Austin, she began by bringing treatments to clients from her apartment while she earned the licensing to open a real medspa.

Twenty-three years later, that apartment has become Beauty Zone Austin — a medical-director-supervised practice built for Austin's Latin community, where Cinthia still leads with the same hands that started it all.

“Passion is the engine that drives my life, and my profession is what I'm truly passionate about. I am happy to have the satisfaction of a happy patient who is satisfied with my work.” — Cinthia Padilla, Founder

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a consultation before booking?

Most treatments can be booked directly online. For Botox, filler, and post-op recovery plans, we recommend starting with a consultation so your plan is built around your goals and health history.

Is Beauty Zone Austin medically supervised?

Yes. Beauty Zone Austin operates under medical director supervision, and treatments are performed by trained, licensed staff.

Do you offer post-surgery recovery care?

Yes. Our post-op and lymphatic drainage packages are designed to support recovery after procedures like BBL, tummy tuck, and liposuction. We build a session plan around your surgeon's timeline. Results vary by individual healing and aftercare.

Do you speak Spanish?

Yes, our team provides care in both English and Spanish.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, through Cherry, so you can spread the cost of body sculpting, post-op, or facial packages over time.

Will results look natural?

Every plan is customized to you. We aim for subtle, natural-looking results, never overdone. Individual results vary.
